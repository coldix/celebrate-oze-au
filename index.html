Beautiful pages for beautiful milestones

Personalised celebration pages for birthdays, anniversaries and life's big moments -- with photo galleries, countdown timers, birthday wishes, video tributes and RSVP.

Everything you need to celebrate

🎂
Beautiful Design
Elegant, personalised pages with photos, dark/light mode and mobile-friendly layout.
⏳
Countdown Timer
Live countdown to the big day -- days, hours, minutes and seconds.
📸
Photo Gallery
Embed a Google Photos album or upload photos directly. Then and now photos in the hero.
🎬
Video Tribute
Embed a YouTube birthday video tribute, compiled from photos and messages sent in.
🎵
Birthday Song
Play a personalised song created with Suno AI -- a unique gift for the milestone birthday.
✉️
RSVP Form
Integrated RSVP with drink preferences, bring-a-plate, dietary needs and more.
💬
Wishes Wall
A public wall of birthday messages from family and friends -- printed as a keepsake.
✏️
Easy to Customise
All event details in a single JSON file -- edit once, updates everywhere on the page.
